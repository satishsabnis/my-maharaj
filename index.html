🍽️ Table Etiquettes
🙏 The Traditional Thali
In Maharashtra, the thali is arranged with rice or chapati in the center. Dal is placed to the upper left, sabzi to the upper right. Pickle and papad are placed on the right edge. The meal begins with a small prayer of gratitude.
🤲 Eating with Hands
In South Indian and Konkani tradition, eating with the right hand is considered respectful and is said to enhance the experience of food. The left hand is kept clean and used only to pour water or pass dishes.
🍃 The Banana Leaf Tradition
In Kerala and Tamil Nadu, meals are served on banana leaves during festivals and special occasions. The leaf is placed with the tip pointing left. After eating, fold the leaf away from you to signal satisfaction.
👴 Serving Order
Traditionally, the eldest members of the family are served first. In a formal setting, guests are served before family members. The host eats last, ensuring all guests are satisfied.
🥛 The Role of Curd and Buttermilk
In Maharashtrian and Konkani traditions, the meal ends with curd rice or a glass of buttermilk (taak). This signals the completion of the meal and aids digestion.
🎨 Traditional Plating
🍛 Maharashtrian Thali Arrangement
Center: Rice or Chapati. Top-left: Dal or Amti. Top-right: Dry Sabzi. Bottom-left: Gravy Sabzi. Bottom-right: Koshimbir (salad). Right edge: Pickle, Papad, Lemon. Top-center: Sweet dish on festival days.
🍃 South Indian Sadya (Banana Leaf)
Top of leaf from left: Banana chips, Pappadam, Pickle, Salt, Raw Mango. Center: Rice served fresh. Around rice: Sambar, Rasam, Kootu, Avial, Thoran. End of meal: Payasam (dessert).
🎨 Garnishing Tips
Dal: finish with a tadka of ghee, cumin and red chilli. Rice dishes: garnish with fresh coriander and a lemon wedge. Curries: a swirl of cream or coconut milk adds visual appeal. Desserts: crushed nuts and saffron strands elevate any sweet.
🌿 Fresh Garnishes Always Available in Dubai
Carrefour and Spinneys consistently stock: fresh coriander, curry leaves, green chillies, ginger, garlic. All Lulu hypermarkets carry: fresh coconut, raw mango (seasonal), banana leaves (by request at some stores).
